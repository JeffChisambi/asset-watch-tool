Dashboard Overview

The dashboard should immediately answer these questions:

How much rent has been collected?
How much is still outstanding?
Which tenants have overdue balances?
Which lease payments are due soon?
Which reminders have been sent today?
1. Summary Cards

These provide a quick overview of the current financial status.

Card	Description
Total Outstanding Balance	Total amount owed by all tenants
Payments Due This Month	Number of tenants expected to pay this month
Overdue Payments	Number of overdue tenant accounts
Payment Reminders Sent	Reminders sent today or this month
Collected This Month	Total rent received during the current month
Upcoming Lease Renewals	Tenants whose lease period is nearing expiry

Example:

Outstanding Balance    MWK 8,450,000

Collected This Month   MWK 23,700,000

Overdue Tenants        16

Due This Week          9

Reminders Sent         28

Lease Renewals         5

Additional Features

To make the Finance/Admin dashboard more useful, consider adding these capabilities:

Automated Reminder Scheduling: Configure reminders to be sent a set number of days before the due date, on the due date, and at regular intervals after the due date until payment is received.
Partial Payment Tracking: Allow staff to record partial payments while automatically recalculating the remaining balance.
Invoice and Receipt Management: Generate rent invoices, issue receipts for payments, and resend them to tenants when needed.
Collection Analytics: Show monthly collection rates, outstanding debt trends, and payment performance by property to help management monitor cash flow.
Tenant Account Statements: Generate a complete statement showing charges, payments, outstanding balances, and reminder history for each tenant.
